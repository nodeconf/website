Adventure time

Join us at Walker Creek Ranch for an intimate un-conference.

NodeConf Adventure is a 3 day event for the whole family. We have a Kids Track and Significant Others track and activities all day and night for the Node community.

All tickets to Adventure include meals and some sort of accommodation in either shared lodges and dormitories or an outside BYOT (bring-your-own-tent) area.

Adventure is an un-conference. Attendees build a schedule of discussions at the event. There are not traditional "talks" and "speakers."
