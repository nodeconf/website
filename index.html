NodeConf is the longest running community driven conference for the Node community. Each year has featured the industry's leading speakers and been a launching point for everything from NodeBots to NodeSchool. This is a special year and will feature the latest in io.js, IoT, and deployment technologies.

Adventure time

Join us at Walker Creek Ranch for an intimate hands-on event. NodeConf Adventure is a 3 day event for the whole family. We have a Kids Track and Significant Others track and activities all day and night for the Node community. All tickets to Adventure include meals and some sort of accommodation in either shared lodges and dormitories or an outside BYOT (bring-your-own-tent) area.

Adventure is an un-conference. Attendees can signup to give workshops and lead discussions now or at the event.
